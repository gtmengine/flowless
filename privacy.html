Privacy Policy

Last updated - October 27, 2025

Flowless is built with a single purpose - reduce distraction on social platforms by hiding infinite feeds and showing a short inspirational quote instead. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data. There are no analytics, ads, user accounts, or tracking pixels.

What we do not collect

  • No personally identifiable information
  • No browsing history or page content
  • No keystrokes, clicks, or behavioral data
  • No device or location data
  • No authentication information
  • No financial information

Data processed locally

All functionality runs locally in your browser. The extension modifies feed containers on user selected sites to display a static quote element. Page content is not read or sent anywhere.

Permissions used

  • storage - saves non-personal preferences like per-site on/off state, last quote ID, and simple UI settings. Values may sync with your Chrome profile if you enable sync.
  • scripting - injects a small deterministic content script on domains you enable to hide the feed and render the quote. It does not capture or transmit content or inputs.

Network requests

Flowless does not execute remote code. All code is bundled in the extension package. No network requests are made to external servers. No data is transmitted or uploaded.

Data retention and deletion

We store nothing on servers. Local preferences remain on your device until you clear extension data or uninstall. To delete preferences - right click the extension icon → Remove from Chrome, or clear site data for the extension in Chrome settings.

Third-party data sharing

We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties. We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the extension's single purpose. We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

Children

Flowless is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data.

Contact

Questions or requests - create an issue on GitHub.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy for clarity or compliance. Updates will be posted at https://gtmengine.github.io/flowless/privacy.html with a revised date.
